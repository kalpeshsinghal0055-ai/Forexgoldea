About ForexGoldEA

Last updated: 14 July 2026

ForexGoldEA is an automated gold-trading tool built by BBFxAi LLC. This page explains who we are, what we build, and how to reach us.

Who we are

BBFxAi LLC builds rules-based, automated trading tools focused on gold (XAU/USD). Our work centres on expert advisors (EAs) for the MetaTrader 4 and MetaTrader 5 platforms — software that executes a defined strategy automatically, with the risk controls the trader sets.

What we do

We develop and share ForexGoldEA, an expert advisor engineered specifically around the behaviour of the XAU/USD market. Alongside the tool, we publish practical guides on our blog covering setup, market sessions, and how gold moves — written to be genuinely useful rather than promotional.

The EA is made available free of charge to traders who open a trading account through our partner broker. We earn an affiliate commission from the broker when you open and fund an account through our links, at no additional cost to you. This is how the project is funded.

Transparency

Trading gold on margin carries a high level of risk, and we don't pretend otherwise. We make no promises of guaranteed profit, and we don't publish fake testimonials. Where we reference performance, our aim is to point to verifiable sources rather than screenshots — you can review a verified track record on our Myfxbook profile.
